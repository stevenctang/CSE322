  • Theodore Evelyn Mosby, also know as Ted Mosby, is one the main characters in the show who is played by Josh Radnor. In the show he is the narrator of the story of how he met his wife. He tells this story to his kids, and from time to time the audience is able to hear him tell his stories. During the series, he dates numerous women who's not the mom, but at the end of some episodes he gives us hints that the mother was in the same room or area as he was.
  • Barney Stinson is the joker or the laid back person of the group who is played by Neil Patrick Harris. Barney always wears expensive suits and loves to drink. Also, he loves to make plans in order to have sex with as many women as possible, he stores these plans in his "Playbook". One of the most memorable quotes that Barney has said was: Suit up, because this list is going to be LEGEN...wait for it...DARY!
  • Marshall Eriksen, played by Jason Segel, is Lily's husband and Ted's best friend. Marshall is very interested with the paranormal and unreal creatures like Sasquatch. Early in the show, he is a law student who dreams to become a lawyer. Later in the show, he become a lawyer where later he becomes a judge.
  • Robin Scherbatsky, played by Cobie Smulders, is a Canadian woman whom before she moved to the United States, was a pop-star. Once she moves to the United States, she becomes a journalist. Later she got a job hosting the morning show on the Channel 12 news show.
  • Lily Aldrin, played by Alyson Hannigan, is the wif of Marshall Eriken and Robin's best friend. In the show she was a kindergarten teacher, but later she becomes an art consultant for The Captain. She met Marshall and Ted in college where she majored in art. Out of everyone in the group, she is a type of person who's extremely sexual just like Barney even though she doesn't look like it.
